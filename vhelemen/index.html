+ mk5/vhelemen

"Vhelemen" is a fictional language created by Maeve Kaori Honji Elizio de Souza, for her species called the "Vhelemen" or "Elemental". Who are people made of magic and elements.

This language is still in its infancy, and is expected to suffer a lot of changes as time moves on.

It has two variants: "ancient" and "modern", the website uses the "modern" variant of Vhelemen language, so it's a lot simpler to type and learn how to type.

Writing things here isn't as accurate as a translator, but it's fun to look at how Vhelemen looks like in roman letters or the other way around!
